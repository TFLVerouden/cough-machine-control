Cuttingoffbranchesexample.py 
The visuals are made with visuals_average and visuals_average_average
THe difference is that visuals_average_average only shows the average filament for each time step,
 whereas visuals_average shows all filaments per timesteps
 contourmap is the basic version to show all widths as a contourmap on an 2D imshow (time vs width), where the amount of pixels is the count.
 Contourmapv2 does the same, but a bit fancier. This was used for the Montpellier presentations.
